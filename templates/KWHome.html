Welcome to KeyWriter!

This is a place where beginners and experts can come together to create wonderful books without the hassle of going through a publisher! To begin click My Library to create your very own new book.

Once it is created it will be saved forever in your library to edit to your hearts content! (Unless of course you want to delete it that's totally fine too!) When you're finished with your book make sure to Save and Publish it so you can keep a PDF of it safe on your computer.

KeyWriter takes your book and converts to LaTeX to give it a more professional look that you wouldn't be able to achieve unless you learnt how to code in LaTeX. But who wants that! KeyWriter makes it easier for people who don't know code or want to waste their time learning it.

So, what are you reading this for? Get over to Your Library and start writing!
