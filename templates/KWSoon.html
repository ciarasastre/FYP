What is Community?

Community will allow users from accross KeyWriter to share their books and have a chance at reading other users books! You will be able to like, favourite and store books for reading later.

With the new community features you have the possibility of growing your own fanbase of people who love your stories!

You will also be able to follow your favourite authors on KeyWriter so you are updated on when they create a new book!

Stay updated as community will be on its way soon!
